About the College

National Management Degree College (NMDC) is a private higher-education institution located on Merchant Road, Botahtaung Township, Yangon, Myanmar. Originally established in 2004 as National Management College (NMC), it was upgraded to a degree-granting college in 2016, becoming NMDC.

NMDC focuses on management-oriented, job-ready programs, with an emphasis on practical skills rather than purely theoretical study. The college is known for its compact, student-friendly campus and its strong connection to the local business environment through partnerships with industry professionals and regular career fairs.

NMDC is recognised within Myanmar's private higher-education system and is a member of the South and Southeast Asian University Network (S&SEASUN), connecting it to higher-education institutions across the region. It promotes research, community service, and regional development as part of its long-term mission.

Campus Life at NMDC

A compact, student-friendly campus in central Yangon

Student Community

NMDC has a tight-knit, friendly student community. Small class sizes mean strong relationships with lecturers and classmates — an environment where every student is known, not just a number.

Prime City Location

Located on Merchant Road in Botahtaung — one of Yangon's most accessible areas — students enjoy easy access to business districts, transport links, and internship and job opportunities across the city.

Career & Industry Events

From career fairs and company visits to guest lectures by business professionals, NMDC regularly brings the industry onto campus — giving students a head start in their professional lives.

Career Opportunities

Typical pathways for NMDC graduates — entering Yangon's private and service sectors

Business & Office Manager

Operations assistants, office managers, admin staff, and sales or marketing executives in SMEs and trading companies across Yangon.

Customer Service & Call-Centre

Customer-service representatives, call-centre agents, and front-desk staff in export/import, service, and travel firms — using professional English skills.

Hotel & Tourism Staff

Hotel front-desk officers, reservations staff, guest-service roles, travel agency assistants, tour-operator staff, and event support across Myanmar's tourism sector.

Junior Reporter & Content Writer

Online media reporters, social-media content creators, PR and content assistants in NGOs, media houses, and small agencies across Myanmar.

English Tutor & Trainer

Teachers or tutors of business English at private language centres, tuition classes, and corporate training programmes throughout Myanmar.

HR & Operations Assistant

Human resources assistants, finance support staff, and operations coordinators — paths that open into broader management and leadership roles over time.
