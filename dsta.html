About the Academy

The Defence Services Technological Academy (DSTA), established in 1993 in Pyin Oo Lwin, is Myanmar's premier technological military academy. It specializes in engineering education for future Armed Forces technical officers. DSTA offers Bachelor of Engineering degrees in Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Computer, and Electronics Engineering.

The academy provides rigorous 5-year programs combining military training with advanced technical education. Graduates are commissioned as technical officers in the Myanmar Armed Forces, serving in engineering and technology leadership roles.
