🏔️ About SAI Lungpi

Established as a **newly established State Agricultural Institute** in Lungpi village, Falam Township, Chin State, SAI Lungpi serves the upland agricultural needs of western Myanmar's highest state. The institute features nutrition-sensitive agriculture curriculum developed with Myanmar Institute for Integrated Development (MIID), focusing on Chin Hills' unique terraced farming and highland crop systems.

With 85+ acres of demonstration hill farms, SAI Lungpi offers 3-year diploma programs featuring 70% practical training in sloping agricultural land technology (SALT), highland vegetables, and integrated farming systems perfect for Chin State's challenging terrain and climate.
