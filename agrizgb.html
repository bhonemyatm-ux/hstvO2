About (State Agricultural Institute)

Zwegabin SAI serves Kayin State's hill and valley agriculture, training in upland rice, coffee, tea, hill fruits, and agroforestry systems adapted to mountainous terrain. Programs focus on slash-and-burn alternatives, terracing techniques, and cash crop development for ethnic minority farming communities in eastern Myanmar.
