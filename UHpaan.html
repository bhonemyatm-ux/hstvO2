About the University

Hpa-An University is a state-run arts and science university serving students from Kayin State and surrounding regions. As a regional higher education institution under Myanmar's Ministry of Education, it provides accessible university education to students who qualify through the national matriculation examination system.

The university focuses on foundational undergraduate programs in arts, sciences, and economics, supporting Kayin State's educational development and serving as an important academic center for local communities.
