About NPI

Naypyitaw Polytechnic Institute (NPI) is a TVET/polytechnic institute mentioned by the Department of Technical and Vocational Education and Training (DTVET) under the Ministry of Science and Technology.

Official public information about NPI’s exact campus address, program list, and entry requirements is limited online; for the most accurate details, contact DTVET directly.
