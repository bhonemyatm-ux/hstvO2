About the University

သူနာပြု တက္ကသိုလ် (မန္တလေး) သည် အထက်မြန်မာပြည်၏ ပထမဆုံးနှင့် အထင်ရှားဆုံးသော သူနာပြုပညာသင်ကြားရေးဗဟိုဌာန ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၉၈ ခုနှစ်တွင် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး မန္တလေးတိုင်းဒေသကြီးနှင့် အထက်မြန်မာပြည်တစ်ဝန်း၏ သူနာပြုများကို လေ့ကျင့်ပေးနေပါသည်။

University of Nursing, Mandalay (UNM) was established on 28th November 1998 as Upper Myanmar's first dedicated nursing university. Originally upgraded from a nursing training school in 1996, UNM now offers comprehensive 4-year Bachelor of Nursing Science (B.N.Sc) and Master of Nursing programs. It serves as the premier nursing education hub for Upper Myanmar, producing approximately 100 professional nurses annually.

With strategic affiliations to Mandalay's major teaching hospitals including a 300-bedded teaching hospital and specialized orthopedic facilities, UNM provides exceptional clinical training opportunities. Graduates are equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, serving in hospitals across Myanmar and internationally in Singapore, Thailand, and the Middle East.

Historical Milestones

1996
Nursing Training School moved to new building between 62nd and 63rd Streets, Chanmyathazi Township.
1998
Upgraded to Institute of Nursing with authority to confer 4-year B.N.Sc (Generic) degrees.
2000s
Expanded to University of Nursing status, becoming Upper Myanmar's nursing education hub.
2010s
Launched Master of Nursing programs to advance nursing education and research in Upper Myanmar.
2020
Played key role in COVID-19 response training for nurses across Upper Myanmar's healthcare system.
2025
Continues to serve as the primary nursing education institution for Upper Myanmar's 7+ million population.
