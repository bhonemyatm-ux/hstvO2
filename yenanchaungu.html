About the University

ရေနံချောင်းတက္ကသိုလ် (အင်္ဂလိပ်: Yenangyaung University) သည် မြန်မာနိုင်ငံ၊ မကွေးတိုင်းဒေသကြီး၊ ရေနံချောင်းမြို့တွင် တည်ရှိသည့် တက္ကသိုလ်တစ်ခု ဖြစ်သည်။[၁] ပညာရေး ဝန်ကြီးဌာန၊ အဆင့်မြင့်ပညာဦးစီးဌာန လက်အောက်တွင် ပါဝင်သည်။

Academic Programs

Undergraduate programmes in arts and sciences

📚

Arts Programmes

Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

Arts degrees offered through 6 departments including Myanmar, English, Geography, History, Philosophy, and Oriental Studies.

Duration: 4 Years (Standard) / 5 Years (Honours)
Min. Marks: Matriculation aggregate meeting regional BA cut-off
Prerequisites: Arts combination at matriculation
Myanmar English Geography History Philosophy Oriental Studies
🧪

Science Programmes

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

Science degrees through 6 departments: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Geology, Zoology, and Botany.

Duration: 4 Years (Standard) / 5 Years (Honours)
Min. Marks: Matriculation aggregate meeting regional BSc cut-off
Prerequisites: Science combination at matriculation
Mathematics Physics Chemistry Geology Zoology Botany
🎓

Postgraduate Programmes

Limited MA/MSc Available

Master's programmes offered in select departments for high-performing graduates (primarily research-focused).

Duration: 1-2 Years
Min. Marks: Honours degree with GPA 4+ required
Prerequisites: Bachelor's degree + entrance selection
MA Arts Subjects MSc Science Subjects Research Thesis
