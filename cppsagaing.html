About the University

သဟကရနှင့် စီမံခန့်ခွဲမှု တက္ကသိုလ် (စစ်ကိုင်း) သည် မြန်မာနိုင်ငံ၏ ဦးဆောင်သော သဟကရပညာသင်ကြားရေးတက္ကသိုလ် ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၈၂ ခုနှစ်တွင် သဟကရသင်တန်းကျောင်းအဖြစ် စတင်တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး ၂၀၁၂ ခုနှစ်တွင် တက္ကသိုလ်အဆင့်သို့ မြှင့်တင်ခဲ့ပါသည်။

University of Co-operative and Management, Sagaing (UCMS) traces its distinguished origins to 1982 when it was established as Sagaing Co-operative Training School, serving Chin State, Kachin State, and Sagaing Division. Located in Shwe Thamar Quarter (now Ywahtaung Quarter) on the Monywa-Mandalay Highway Road, Sagaing Township, the institution was upgraded to Co-operative College in 1996.

In 2012, President U Thein Sein's historic visit to Sagaing led to its elevation as Co-operative University, Sagaing — a special university dedicated to cooperative and management education. On 5th November 2021, it was renamed University of Co-operative and Management, Sagaing. UCMS offers Bachelor of Business Science (B.B.Sc.) with five specializations (Commerce, Co-operative Studies, Economics, Management Studies, Statistics), postgraduate diplomas (PGDip), and master's programmes. With 80+ academic staff and 1,200+ students, UCMS accepts 300 students annually and has produced 8,000+ graduates serving as cooperative leaders, social entrepreneurs, business managers, government officials, and development specialists across Myanmar. The university maintains close ties with the Ministry of Co-operatives, cooperative banks, and international development organizations.

About the University

သဟကရနှင့် စီမံခန့်ခွဲမှု တက္ကသိုလ် (စစ်ကိုင်း) သည် မြန်မာနိုင်ငံ၏ ဦးဆောင်သော သဟကရပညာသင်ကြားရေးတက္ကသိုလ် ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၈၂ ခုနှစ်တွင် သဟကရသင်တန်းကျောင်းအဖြစ် စတင်တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး ၂၀၁၂ ခုနှစ်တွင် တက္ကသိုလ်အဆင့်သို့ မြှင့်တင်ခဲ့ပါသည်။

University of Co-operative and Management, Sagaing (UCMS) traces its distinguished origins to 1982 when it was established as Sagaing Co-operative Training School, serving Chin State, Kachin State, and Sagaing Division. Located in Shwe Thamar Quarter (now Ywahtaung Quarter) on the Monywa-Mandalay Highway Road, Sagaing Township, the institution was upgraded to Co-operative College in 1996.

In 2012, President U Thein Sein's historic visit to Sagaing led to its elevation as Co-operative University, Sagaing — a special university dedicated to cooperative and management education. On 5th November 2021, it was renamed University of Co-operative and Management, Sagaing. UCMS offers Bachelor of Business Science (B.B.Sc.) with five specializations (Commerce, Co-operative Studies, Economics, Management Studies, Statistics), postgraduate diplomas (PGDip), and master's programmes. With 80+ academic staff and 1,200+ students, UCMS accepts 300 students annually and has produced 8,000+ graduates serving as cooperative leaders, social entrepreneurs, business managers, government officials, and development specialists across Myanmar. The university maintains close ties with the Ministry of Co-operatives, cooperative banks, and international development organizations.

Historical Milestones

1972
State-level Co-operative Training School launched in Monywa, Sagaing Division — foundation of cooperative education in Upper Myanmar.
1982
Sagaing Co-operative Training School established on 10th January in Shwe Thamar Quarter, Sagaing — serving Chin, Kachin, and Sagaing regions.
1996
Upgraded to Co-operative College, Sagaing — introduced Commerce and Statistics subjects, expanded programs and faculty.
2012
President U Thein Sein's visit led to elevation as Co-operative University, Sagaing — construction of main building began, Commerce Department founded.
2021
Renamed University of Co-operative and Management, Sagaing (UCMS) on 5th November — reflecting expanded mandate in management and social enterprise education.
2020s
Launched social enterprise management programs, strengthened international partnerships, and expanded applied research in regional development and cooperative studies.
