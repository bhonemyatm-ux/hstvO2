About (State Agricultural Institute)

Pyinmana SAI, one of Myanmar's premier agricultural training institutes, offers comprehensive programs in crop science, horticulture, plant protection, and agricultural mechanization. Located in the capital region, it features extensive research farms and emphasizes modern agricultural technologies, precision farming, and extension services for nationwide application.
