About the University

ရန်ကုန် စီးပွားရေး တက္ကသိုလ် သည် မြန်မာနိုင်ငံ၏ အထင်ရှားဆုံးနှင့် ဦးဆောင်သော စီးပွားရေး၊ စီးပွားရေးလုပ်ငန်း၊ စာရင်းကိုင်နှင့် စီမံခန့်ခွဲမှုပညာသင်ကြားရေးဗဟိုဌာန ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၆၄ ခုနှစ်တွင် တက္ကသိုလ်အဖြစ် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး ၁၉၂၄ ခုနှစ်မှ စတင်သော သမိုင်းကြောင်းရှည်လျားပါသည်။

Yangon University of Economics (YUECO) traces its prestigious origins to 1924 when the Section of Economics was established within Yangon University. In 1964, under the Educational Reform, it became an independent institution — the Yangon Institute of Economics. In 2014, it was upgraded and renamed Yangon University of Economics, solidifying its position as Myanmar's premier institution for economics, business, accounting, statistics, and management education.

YUECO operates two campuses: the main campus in Kamayut Township (postgraduate and executive programs) and the Hlaing Campus (undergraduate programs). With over 7,000 students and 350+ academic staff, YUECO has nurtured more than 76,100 graduates who now serve as economists, statisticians, accountants, business leaders, entrepreneurs, and policymakers shaping Myanmar's economy. The university offers 9 bachelor's degrees, 15 master's degrees (regular and professional), 10 diplomas, and 3 doctoral degrees, making it the most comprehensive business and economics university in Myanmar.

Historical Milestones

1924
Section of Economics established within Yangon University — beginning of economics education in Myanmar.
1964
Reorganized as Yangon Institute of Economics — independent institution for economics, commerce, and statistics education.
1988
Department of Management Studies and Department of Applied Economics established — expanding business education.
2009
Myanmar-India Entrepreneurship Development Centre (MIEDC) inaugurated — fostering entrepreneurship.
2013
Launched MBF (Master of Banking & Finance), DIM (Diploma in Insurance Management), and CAES programs.
2014
Upgraded to Yangon University of Economics (YUECO) — launched MAS (Master of Actuarial Science) and DipRS (Diploma in Revenue Studies).
2015
Hlaing Campus opened — offering BAct, BPA, BDevS, BPS undergraduate programs.
2019
Expanded Hlaing Campus with B.Com, BBA, Economics, and Statistics undergraduate programs.
2020
Launched MMM (Master of Marketing Management), MHTM (Master of Hospitality & Tourism Management), and PGDHRM.
