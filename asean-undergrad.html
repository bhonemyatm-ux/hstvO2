Scholarship Overview

The ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship (AUS) is a prestigious merit-based scholarship offered by Singapore's top autonomous universities - National University of Singapore (NUS), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore Management University (SMU), and Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). This scholarship enables outstanding Myanmar students who have completed high school to pursue full 4-year Bachelor's degree programmes at world-class institutions consistently ranked among Asia's and the world's best universities.

Unlike the MOE ASEAN Scholarship for secondary students, the ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship is administered directly by each university and targets students ready to begin undergraduate studies. Scholars receive comprehensive support including full tuition subsidy, annual living allowance of S$6,000, accommodation allowance or hostel placement, one-time return economy airfare, and a completion grant. The scholarship covers popular programmes including engineering, business, computer science, sciences, social sciences, design, and more at institutions ranked within global top 50.

✨

What Makes ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship Special?

This scholarship offers a complete undergraduate education at Singapore's most prestigious universities - NUS (#8 QS World 2024), NTU (#26), SMU (top Asian business school), and SUTD (innovation-focused engineering). Unlike many scholarships, you can apply to MULTIPLE universities simultaneously to maximize chances! Scholars join diverse international cohorts, access cutting-edge facilities, gain highly-valued degrees recognized worldwide, and benefit from Singapore's strong graduate employment rates (90%+). The 3-year service obligation in Singapore or ASEAN after graduation provides valuable work experience in Asia's business hub!

03

How to Apply

1

Research Universities & Programmes

Visit NUS, NTU, SMU, and SUTD websites to explore undergraduate programmes. Consider your interests, career goals, and programme requirements. Check each university's strengths: NUS (comprehensive), NTU (engineering/tech), SMU (business/social sciences), SUTD (design/engineering innovation).

NUS Scholarships → NTU Scholarships →
2

Prepare Academic Documents

Gather official high school transcripts (all years), graduation certificate or expected graduation letter, English proficiency test scores (IELTS/TOEFL if required), certificates of awards, competitions, co-curricular activities. Ensure all documents are properly certified/notarized!

3

Write Compelling Personal Statement

Craft strong personal statement/essay explaining: Why this university and programme? What are your academic and career goals? How will you contribute to campus community? What leadership experiences demonstrate your potential? Be genuine, specific, and passionate!

4

Complete Online Applications

Create accounts on each university's admissions portal. Complete online application forms carefully with accurate information. Upload all required documents. Select ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship when indicating scholarship preferences. Submit before March 20, 2026!

5

Submit Applications to Multiple Universities

Apply to AT LEAST 2-3 universities to maximize chances! NUS, NTU, SMU, and SUTD evaluate independently - acceptance at one doesn't affect others. Choose different programmes if interests vary. Keep copies of all submissions!

6

Prepare for Potential Interview

If shortlisted, you may be invited for interview (online or in-person). Prepare to discuss: academic interests, career goals, leadership experiences, knowledge about university/programme, why Singapore. Practice common questions, research current affairs, demonstrate enthusiasm!

7

Await Results & Accept Offer

Universities notify successful applicants May-July 2026 via email. If you receive multiple offers, compare programmes, campuses, support services. Accept your chosen offer by deadline, complete enrollment procedures, apply for Student Pass, arrange accommodation, and prepare for departure to Singapore in August!

📌 Important Application Tips:

  • Apply Early: Don't wait until March 20! Submit by January-February to avoid technical issues
  • Multiple Applications: Apply to multiple universities - it's allowed and recommended!
  • Programme Selection: Choose programmes matching your strengths and interests, not just prestige
  • Scholarship Indication: Clearly indicate interest in ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship in application
  • Service Bond Awareness: Understand 3-year service obligation before accepting scholarship
04

Pro Tips for Success

📊

Academic Excellence is Foundation

Aim for top 5-10% of your cohort! Universities seek students with consistently excellent grades across all subjects. Strong performance in Mathematics, Sciences, and English is particularly important. GPA alone isn't enough - show upward trend and challenge yourself with difficult subjects!

🗣️

English Proficiency is Non-Negotiable

ALL programmes taught in English! Practice extensively: read English books/news, watch English media, write essays, speak daily. Take IELTS/TOEFL early to meet requirements. Weak English = automatic rejection regardless of other strengths. Aim for IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 90+!

🏆

Stand Out with Leadership & Activities

Singapore universities value well-rounded students! Hold leadership positions (student council, club president), participate actively in sports/arts/community service, win competitions/olympiads. Don't just join - LEAD and ACHIEVE! Document everything with certificates!

✍️

Personal Statement: Show Your Unique Story

Avoid generic essays! Share YOUR specific journey, genuine passions, clear goals. Explain why this specific university and programme align with your aspirations. Show you've researched thoroughly. Demonstrate maturity, curiosity, and potential to contribute. Get feedback from teachers!

🎯

Strategic Programme Selection

Choose programmes matching your genuine interests AND academic strengths. Highly competitive programmes (Computer Science, Business, Engineering) require exceptional qualifications. Consider less popular but excellent programmes to increase chances. You can always specialize within broader degree!

📝

Apply to Multiple Universities

Apply to ALL FOUR universities if possible! Each evaluates independently with different criteria and capacities. NUS focuses on all-round excellence, NTU on technical aptitude, SMU on leadership/communication, SUTD on creativity/innovation. Diversify your chances!

🎤

Interview Preparation is Critical

If shortlisted, interview performance often determines final selection! Practice common questions, research university thoroughly, prepare examples of leadership/teamwork, stay updated on current affairs, show genuine enthusiasm. Be confident but humble, articulate but concise!

💼

Understand Service Bond Commitment

3-year work obligation in Singapore/ASEAN after graduation is LEGALLY BINDING! This isn't a limitation - it's an opportunity for valuable international work experience! However, if you plan to work elsewhere immediately, consider scholarships without bonds. Breaking bond costs thousands!
