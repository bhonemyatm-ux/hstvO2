About the University

သမဝါယမနှင့် စီမံခန့်ခွဲမှုပညာ တက္ကသိုလ် (သန်လျင်) သည် ရန်ကုန်တိုင်း၏ ဦးဆောင်သော စီးပွားရေးနှင့် လူမှုစွန့်ဦးတည်ထောင်မှုတက္ကသိုလ် ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၉၄ ခုနှစ်တွင် စတင်တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး ၂၀၁၂ ခုနှစ်တွင် တက္ကသိုလ်အဆင့်သို့ မြှင့်တင်ခဲ့ပါသည်။

University of Co-operative and Management, Thanlyin (UCMT) traces its distinguished origins to 19 May 1994 when it was established as Yangon Co-operative Regional College. Located at 8 Miles (13 km from Yangon city center) in Thanlyin Township, Yangon Region, the college was renamed Yangon Co-operative Degree College in 1996 and upgraded to Co-operative University (Thanlyin) in the 2012–2013 academic year.

UCMT is one of Myanmar's best business universities, offering a unique Bachelor of Business Science (B.B.Sc.) with five innovative specializations: Accounting & Finance, Applied Statistics, Marketing Management, Regional Development, and Social Enterprise Management. The university accepts 300 students annually for its four-year degree programmes and also offers postgraduate diplomas (PGDip) and master's programmes in all five specializations. With 100+ academic staff and 1,200+ students, UCMT has produced 7,000+ graduates serving as social entrepreneurs, business leaders, cooperative managers, development specialists, and data analysts across Myanmar and beyond. UCMT is a proud member of the Southeast Asian Social Innovative Network (SEASIN), a European Commission-funded project promoting social innovation in partnership with 15 international universities and organizations.
