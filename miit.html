About the University

Myanmar Institute of Information Technology (MIIT) is a prestigious technological university located in Chanmyathazi Township, Mandalay. It was established in 2015 as a National Centre of Excellence through collaboration between the Myanmar and Indian governments.

Modeled after India's IIIT-Bangalore, MIIT focuses on world-class information technology education with modern curricula, strong infrastructure, practical learning, and updated teaching methods.

The institute operates under the Ministry of Education and serves students from across Myanmar with a focus on research-driven learning in computing, electronics, and engineering fields.

Entrance Requirements & Admission

Student-friendly summary based on available MIIT information.

Undergraduate Admission

Students must pass Myanmar's Matriculation Exam in the admission year, including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English. Admission is competitive and usually includes Matriculation marks plus an entrance test.

Admission Process

  • Apply after Matriculation results with the required subjects.
  • Sit for the entrance exam when announced by MIIT.
  • Selection is merit-based and competitive.
  • The academic year commonly starts in December.
  • Program capacity is around 60 students per program from many applicants.

Postgraduate note: Master's admission usually requires a relevant bachelor's degree such as B.E. (Hons.) from MIIT, UCS universities, or equivalent IT, computer, electronics, or mechatronics degrees from Myanmar or abroad.
