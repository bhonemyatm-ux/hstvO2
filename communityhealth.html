About the University

အခြေခံ ကျန်းမာရေး တက္ကသိုလ် (မကွေး) သည် မြန်မာနိုင်ငံ၏ တစ်ခုတည်းသော အခြေခံကျန်းမာရေးတက္ကသိုလ် ဖြစ်ပြီး ကျေးလက်ဒေသများတွင် အဓိက ကျန်းမာရေးဝန်ဆောင်မှုပေးသူများကို လေ့ကျင့်ပေးနေပါသည်။ ၁၉၅၁ ခုနှစ်တွင် စတင်တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး ၁၉၉၅ ခုနှစ်တွင် တက္ကသိုလ်အဆင့်သို့ မြှင့်တင်ခဲ့ပါသည်။

University of Community Health, Magway (UCH) traces its proud origins to 1951 when it was first established as the Health Assistant Training School in Yangon. In December 1991, it merged with the Aung San Health Demonstration Unit (Field Department) to become the School of Health Science for Basic Health Workers. The institution was upgraded to university status in 1995, becoming Myanmar's only community health university.

In 1999, the military government relocated UCH from Yangon Division to its current 41.4-hectare (102-acre) campus at Htonpauk, 14.5 km north of Magway city. UCH is the only university in Myanmar that produces Basic Health Workers (Health Assistants), who serve as the backbone of primary healthcare in rural Myanmar where access to regular physicians is limited. Graduates are licensed to practice in the Department of Public Health and FDA across the country.

Historical Milestones

1951
Health Assistant Training School established in Yangon — the founding seed of UCH.
1963
School relocated to Insein, expanding training capacity.
1968
Moved to Hlegu for better field training access.
1989
Came under purview of Ministry of Health — strategic shift to national health workforce development.
1991
Merged with Aung San Health Demonstration Unit to become School of Health Science for Basic Health Workers.
1995
Upgraded to University of Community Health — began offering 4-year bachelor's degree.
2000
Relocated to current 102-acre campus at Htonpauk, Magway — serving Upper Myanmar.
2006
Field Department moved to Tawsaint village, Salin Township for expanded rural training.
2017
Launched Master of Community Health (MComm.H) program for advanced public health training.
