How It Works

What happens after you submit

Simple, transparent, and fast — here's exactly what the team does with every report or collaboration request.

1

You Submit

Fill in the short Google Form. Takes about 2 minutes. You can stay anonymous or leave contact details for follow-up.

2

We Review

The HST team reads every submission within 48 hours and checks it against our data sources or discusses internally.

3

We Update or Respond

Confirmed corrections go live on the site. Collaboration requests get a personal reply from the team within 3 days.

Why It Matters

Your input keeps HST accurate

HST is maintained by a small student team. Community reports are the fastest way we find and fix issues.

Anonymous Option

You never have to share your name. Submit with complete privacy if you prefer — we only ask for contact if you want a reply.

Fast Turnaround

Reports are reviewed within 48 hours. Corrections to live data are usually published the same day they're confirmed.

Real Team Response

Collaboration requests get a personal message from the HST team — not an automated reply. We read and respond to all serious proposals.
