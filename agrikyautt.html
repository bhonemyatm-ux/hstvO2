🌾 About SAI Kyauktaw

Located in Rakhine State's Kyauktaw Township along the Kaladan River, State Agricultural Institute Kyauktaw specializes in coastal agriculture for western Myanmar's unique agro-climatic zone. The institute offers 3-year diploma programs with 70% practical training on 75+ acres of demonstration farms focusing on paddy, pulses, coastal horticulture, and riverine crops.

Recognized at high government levels (SAC member visits), SAI Kyauktaw serves Rakhine State's agricultural development needs with modern facilities and experienced faculty serving the coastal plains and river valleys.
