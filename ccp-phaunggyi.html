About the College

သမဝါယမ ကောလိပ် (ဖောင်ကြီး) သည် အောက်မြန်မာပြည်၏ ဦးဆောင်သော သမဝါယမပညာသင်ကြားရေးကောလိပ် ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၉၆ ခုနှစ်တွင် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး သမဝါယမမန်နေဂျာများ၊ စိုက်ပျိုးရေးကောလိပ်ခေါင်းဆောင်များနှင့် ကျေးလက်ဖွံ့ဖြိုးရေးကျွမ်းကျင်သူများကို လေ့ကျင့်ပေးနေပါသည်။

Co-operative College (Phaunggyi), commonly known as CCP, was established in 1996 as Lower Myanmar's premier institution dedicated to cooperative education with a special focus on agricultural cooperation. Located in the peaceful rural setting of Phaunggyi Village, Shwepyitha Township, Bago Region (near the Yangon-Mandalay Highway), CCP serves as the cooperative education hub for Bago, Yangon, Ayeyarwady, and Mon States.

Operating under the Ministry of Co-operatives, CCP offers bachelor's degree programs in cooperative management, agricultural cooperation, business administration, and cooperative banking, as well as diploma courses for working cooperative professionals. With 40+ academic staff and 600+ students, CCP has produced 4,000+ graduates serving as cooperative managers, agricultural cooperative leaders, cooperative bank officers, and rural development specialists across Lower Myanmar. The college is distinguished by its international exchange program with Daegu University (South Korea) and its strong emphasis on agricultural cooperation, making it the preferred choice for students interested in agribusiness and rural development.
