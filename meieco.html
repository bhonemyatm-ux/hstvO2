About the University

မိတ္ထီလာ စီးပွားရေး တက္ကသိုလ် သည် အလယ်ပိုင်းနှင့် မြောက်ပိုင်းမြန်မာပြည်၏ အထင်ရှားဆုံး စီးပွားရေး၊ စီးပွားရေးလုပ်ငန်း၊ စာရင်းကိုင်နှင့် စီမံခန့်ခွဲမှုပညာသင်ကြားရေးဗဟိုဌာန ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၂၀၀၁ ခုနှစ် ဇွန်လ ၁၄ ရက်တွင် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပါသည်။

Meiktila University of Economics (MEUE) was established on 14th June 2001 as a professional state university under the Department of Higher Education (Mandalay Region), Ministry of Education. Located in the strategic central city of Meiktila, Mandalay Region, MEUE is one of only three universities of economics and business in Myanmar, alongside Yangon University of Economics and Monywa University of Economics.

MEUE was founded with a noble mission: to train economists, statisticians, accountants, and management personnel, and to conduct research on economic, business, and statistical issues related to the Myanmar economy. With 175+ full-time staff (81 engaged in teaching and research) and over 3,500 students, MEUE has produced 18,000+ graduates serving as economists, accountants, bankers, civil servants, and entrepreneurs across Central and Northern Myanmar. The university offers bachelor's, master's (including full-time MBA, MPA, MBF), and PhD programs in commerce, business administration, economics, statistics, and public administration.
