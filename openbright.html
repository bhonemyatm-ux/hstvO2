Scholarship Overview

Ho Chi Minh City Open University (HCMC OU) is one of Vietnam's leading public universities, established in 1990 and located in the vibrant economic capital of Vietnam. The university offers the Open Bright Scholarship programme specifically designed for international students, including Myanmar nationals, who demonstrate both academic merit and strong commitment to community service. This prestigious scholarship provides 100% tuition fee waiver for the entire 5-year duration of bachelor's degree programmes taught in English.

HCMC OU is recognized for quality education in Business Administration, Information Technology, Tourism & Hospitality, Accounting, Finance, Marketing, and other practical fields highly relevant to Southeast Asia's dynamic economy. The university serves over 60,000 students and has established partnerships with universities worldwide. Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam's largest city and economic powerhouse, offers students exposure to international business, technology companies, startups, and cultural diversity. Living costs in HCMC are very affordable compared to other Asian cities, and the city is just a short flight from Myanmar, making it convenient for students to maintain family connections.

✨

Why Open Bright Scholarship is Excellent for Myanmar Students

This scholarship is PERFECT for Myanmar students with community service records! 100% tuition waiver for FULL 5 YEARS - no annual renewal stress! Study in Vietnam - close to Myanmar (just 1-2 hour flight), similar culture (Buddhist majority, rice culture, friendly people), affordable living costs ($300-500/month), safe city, delicious food! English-taught programmes in practical fields: Business, IT, Tourism - skills needed in Myanmar! HCMC is Southeast Asia's startup hub - amazing internship and job opportunities. Community service is VALUED in selection - if you've volunteered, this scholarship prioritizes YOU! Myanmar students already studying here successfully. After graduation, can work in Vietnam or return to Myanmar with valuable ASEAN business experience!

03

How to Apply

1

Research Programmes & Requirements

Visit HCMC OU website (ou.edu.vn) and explore English-taught bachelor's programmes: Business, IT, Tourism, etc. Check specific admission requirements, programme structure, career outcomes. Decide which programme aligns with your interests and goals. Consider HCMC living costs and prepare budget.

2

Prepare English Proficiency

Take IELTS (target 5.5+) or TOEFL iBT (target 60+). If you don't have test scores, you can take HCMC OU's English placement test upon arrival. Alternatively, start English preparation courses now to improve proficiency. English proficiency crucial for success in programme!

3

Document Community Service Record

THIS IS CRITICAL! Gather ALL evidence of volunteer work and community service: (1) Certificates from organizations, (2) Recommendation letters from supervisors/community leaders, (3) Photos of activities, (4) Detailed descriptions of projects and your roles, (5) Impact statements showing outcomes of your service. Create portfolio showcasing community engagement!

4

Gather Academic Documents

Collect high school transcripts, diploma, exam certificates. Get notarized English translations if documents in Burmese. Ensure all documents officially stamped/sealed by your school. Prepare passport copy. Get passport-size photos taken.

5

Write Compelling Personal Statement

Write essay explaining: (1) Why you chose your programme, (2) Your community service experience and impact, (3) How HCMC OU education will help you continue serving communities, (4) Career goals and plans. Emphasize community commitment - align with "Open Bright" scholarship's values of social contribution!

6

Submit Online Application

Visit HCMC OU International Student Portal (sdh.ou.edu.vn/en) or contact International Office (email: admission@ou.edu.vn). Complete online application form. Upload all documents. Submit scholarship application specifically requesting "Open Bright Scholarship" - explain why you deserve it based on community service! Pay application fee if required.

Visit Admission Portal →
7

Await Admission & Scholarship Decision

University reviews application (4-6 weeks). If accepted, you'll receive admission letter AND scholarship decision. If awarded Open Bright Scholarship, congratulations - 100% tuition covered for 5 years! If not awarded scholarship initially, can still accept admission and try for other scholarships or pay tuition.

8

Apply for Vietnam Student Visa

With admission letter, apply for Vietnam student visa: (1) University provides visa invitation letter, (2) Apply at Vietnam Embassy in Myanmar (Yangon), (3) Submit passport, photos, admission letter, financial proof (bank statement showing living cost capacity), (4) Visa processing 5-10 business days. Student visa typically 1 year, renewable annually.

9

Prepare for Departure & Arrival

Book flight to Ho Chi Minh City (Yangon-HCMC ~2 hours), arrange temporary accommodation for first week (hostel/hotel ~$10-20/night), bring cash (USD or VND) for initial expenses, connect with Myanmar student community in HCMC (Facebook groups), prepare for orientation week. University helps with airport pickup and settlement!

📌 Critical Application Tips:

  • Community Service Documentation: This is your competitive edge - invest time documenting thoroughly!
  • Contact International Office Early: Email admission@ou.edu.vn with questions - they're very responsive
  • Apply Early: Better scholarship consideration for early applicants
  • Budget Planning: Even with full tuition scholarship, prepare $4,000-6,000/year for living costs
  • Myanmar Student Network: Connect with current Myanmar students at HCMC OU for advice!
04

Pro Tips for Success

🤝

Community Service is Your Golden Ticket

Open Bright Scholarship SPECIFICALLY values community engagement! If you've done ANY volunteer work - teaching, orphanage visits, environmental projects, helping elderly, youth mentoring, charity events - document EVERYTHING! Quality matters: show impact, leadership, sustained commitment. One student got scholarship for teaching English to village children 2 years. Another for organizing environmental cleanups. Your service makes you stand out!

💰

HCMC Living Costs Are VERY Affordable

Full tuition scholarship means you only fund living expenses - very manageable! Budget breakdown: Dormitory $50-100/month (cheapest option), Food $150-250/month (eat like local at street food stalls $1-2/meal), Transportation $30/month (bus/metro), Personal $50-100/month. TOTAL: $300-500/month! Many Myanmar students work part-time (English tutoring $10-15/hour) to support themselves. HCMC is 1/3 cost of Bangkok!

🇻🇳

Vietnam is Culturally Similar to Myanmar

Vietnam and Myanmar share many cultural similarities: Buddhist traditions, rice-based cuisine (pho vs mohinga!), family values, respect for elders, tropical climate. Vietnamese people very friendly to Myanmar nationals - strong ASEAN solidarity! Food is delicious and similar flavors to Myanmar cuisine. No major culture shock. Large Myanmar community in HCMC provides support network. You'll feel at home!

💼

HCMC Career Opportunities Are Excellent

Ho Chi Minh City is Southeast Asia's rising economic powerhouse! Internship opportunities: multinational companies (Samsung, Intel, Unilever), startups, Vietnamese corporations, NGOs. Many HCMC OU students intern during studies (credit-bearing). After graduation, can work in Vietnam (student visa converts to work visa) or return to Myanmar with valuable ASEAN business experience. Vietnam-Myanmar business ties growing - bilingual graduates highly valued!

📚

Programme Selection Strategy

Choose programme based on Vietnam's strengths AND Myanmar's needs: Business Administration (Vietnam's business practices applicable to Myanmar), IT (Vietnam's tech sector booming - learn cutting-edge skills), Tourism (Vietnam #1 ASEAN tourist destination - excellent training!), International Business (perfect for Vietnam-Myanmar trade). Avoid overly theoretical programmes - HCMC OU emphasizes practical skills employers value!

🗣️

English Proficiency Pathway

If your English below IELTS 5.5, don't worry! HCMC OU offers: (1) English placement test on campus (cheaper than IELTS), (2) Foundation English courses (3-6 months intensive), (3) Conditional admission (study English while starting degree). Vietnamese students also learning English - you're not alone! Focus on communication skills, not perfect grammar. Practical English matters most!

🏠

Accommodation Options

HCMC OU doesn't have extensive on-campus housing, but many affordable options: (1) University dormitory (if available, ~$50-100/month, shared room), (2) Private student hostels near campus ($80-150/month), (3) Shared apartment with other students ($100-200/month per person), (4) Homestay with Vietnamese family ($150-250/month, includes meals, great for language!). Most Myanmar students share apartments - cheaper and fun!

✈️

Stay Connected to Myanmar

HCMC only 1-2 hours flight to Yangon - easy to visit family during holidays! Flights affordable ($100-200 round-trip on budget airlines). Vietnamese SIM cards very cheap ($5-10/month with data) - video call family anytime. Myanmar restaurants in HCMC's District 1 and 5 (mohinga, laphet thoke available!). Active Myanmar student associations organize cultural events. You can maintain Myanmar connection while studying!
