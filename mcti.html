About MCTI

Military Computer & Technological Institute (MCTI) is located in Hopong Station under the Eastern Command in Shan State. Public reports describe MCTI as a defence services technology/engineering training institute and note it was moved to Hopong on 22 October 2002.

Why Choose MCTI?

💻

Technology + Engineering Skills

Training focuses on improving skills in military engineering/technology-related subjects.

🪖

Military Career Path

As a defence services institute, it supports career development within military units and battalions.

📍

Eastern Command Location

Based in Hopong (Pa-O Self-Administered Zone area) in southern Shan State.
