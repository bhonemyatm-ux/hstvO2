Admission Process

General admission requirements for education colleges in Myanmar

📝 Basic Requirements

  • Pass Myanmar Matriculation Examination
  • Meet minimum mark requirements (typically 300-350 marks)
  • Myanmar citizenship
  • Good health and physical fitness
  • Interest in teaching profession

📅 Application Timeline

  • Applications typically open after Matriculation results
  • Check with Ministry of Education for exact dates
  • Admissions coordinated through MoE SMS system

💡 How to Apply

  • Register through Ministry of Education SMS application system
  • Select education colleges in your preferred region
  • Submit required documents to assigned college
  • Wait for admission confirmation

Middle School Teacher

Avg: 250,000 - 500,000 MMK/month
