🌴 About SAI Dawei

Situated in the tropical coastal region of Tanintharyi, State Agricultural Institute Dawei specializes in plantation agriculture perfect for southern Myanmar's climate. With 80+ acres of rubber, coconut, cashew, and fruit plantations, SAI Dawei offers comprehensive 3-year diploma programs featuring 70% practical training on working commercial plantations.

The coastal location provides unique opportunities for integrated coastal agriculture, aquaculture, and tropical fruit production training tailored to Dawei District's agricultural economy.
