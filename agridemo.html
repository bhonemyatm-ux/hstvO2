🏔️ About SAI Demoso

Nestled in the scenic Kayah hill tracts, State Agricultural Institute Demoso specializes in upland agriculture perfect for Kayah State's highland climate. With 90+ acres of terraced hill farms, coffee plantations, and temperate fruit orchards, SAI Demoso delivers 3-year diploma programs featuring intensive practical training in hill farming techniques.

Perfect for students seeking careers in upland agriculture, coffee production, and hill livestock management in Kayah's unique highland ecosystem.
