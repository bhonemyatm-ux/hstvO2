About the University

ဆေးဝါး တက္ကသိုလ် (မန္တလေး) သည် အထက်မြန်မာပြည်၏ တစ်ခုတည်းသော ဆေးဝါးပညာသင်ကြားရေးဗဟိုဌာန ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၂၀၀၀ ခုနှစ်တွင် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး ၂၀၀၆ ခုနှစ်တွင် တက္ကသိုလ်အဆင့်သို့ မြှင့်တင်ခဲ့ပါသည်။

University of Pharmacy, Mandalay (UOPM) was established on 22nd May 2000 as the Institute of Pharmacy, Mandalay with its first batch of 163 matriculated students. Initially housed temporarily in the former Ba Htoo hostel at the Institute of Medicine, Mandalay campus, it moved to the former Mental Hospital on 35th Street (between 65th and 66th) on 25th June 2000.

On 4th May 2004, UOPM relocated to its present peaceful location on Htonebo-Myitnge two-way road near Taw-twin village, Amarapura Township. The name was officially changed to University of Pharmacy, Mandalay in 2006. The first graduation ceremony was held on 26th June 2004, conferring B.Pharm degrees to 73 graduates. To date, UOPM has produced 1,590+ B.Pharm graduates, 34 M.Pharm graduates, and 6 PhD holders, serving as Upper Myanmar's primary source of qualified pharmacists.
