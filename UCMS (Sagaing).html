About UCMS (Sagaing)

UCMS is a public university that focuses on co-operative and management-related education. Its mission includes developing programs that support regional development and co-operative/social enterprise-related skills, and conducting applied/practical research.

Reliable tip: Always confirm admission rules and program structure from the official UCMS pages linked in the sidebar before applying.

What students should prepare

🗂️

Documents & timeline

Follow the UCMS admission instructions and deadlines from the official Admission page.

📖

Study skills

Expect business-style learning: reading, note-taking, basic math/statistics, presentations, and group work.

✅

Pick a track early

Look at the listed B.B.Sc. specialization areas and choose based on what job you want after graduation.

Programs (Officially listed)

These are the specialization areas UCMS publicly lists for its B.B.Sc. program.

🤝

Social Enterprise Management

B.B.Sc. specialization

Focus on managing co-operative/social-enterprise style organizations.

📍

Regional Development

B.B.Sc. specialization

Focus on development planning, community and regional development work.

📊

Applied Statistics

B.B.Sc. specialization

Focus on data, statistics, and applied analysis used in business/government.

💰

Accounting & Finance

B.B.Sc. specialization

Focus on accounting, finance basics, and financial administration.

Higher Study (Officially listed)

UCMS states that qualified students can continue to a 1-year Postgraduate Diploma and then a 2-year Master’s program. Check the official Admission/Program pages for the latest rules and eligibility.
