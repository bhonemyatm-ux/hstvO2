Mohnyin University

Mohnyin University is a public government university located in Mohnyin, Kachin State, Myanmar. It offers undergraduate programs in arts and science subjects for students from Kachin State and surrounding areas.

The university aims to provide higher education in northern Myanmar’s challenging terrain, with B.A. and B.Sc. degrees primarily taught in Burmese and supported by regional context.

Mohnyin is located in an area with ongoing instability and limited infrastructure, so students should confirm current safety conditions and academic schedules before making plans.

Mohnyin University is a public arts and sciences university located in Mohnyin town, Kachin State, Myanmar. It was established in the early 2010s to expand access to higher education for students in this remote area.

The university offers undergraduate programs such as Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in subjects like languages, history, mathematics, and basic sciences, mainly taught in Burmese.

Because Mohnyin is in a conflict‑affected region, students and parents should carefully check current security, travel conditions, and academic status before considering enrollment or travel to the campus.

Why Choose Mohnyin University?

Local University for Kachin Villages

Students from nearby villages can attend university close to home without moving to big cities.

State ‑ Recognized University

Degrees are recognized nationally, supporting careers in teaching and civil service.

Community‑Oriented Teaching

Teachers focus on local needs, preparing students for village schools and local offices.

Pathway to Further Study

Graduates can transfer or apply to larger universities in Mandalay, Yangon, or Taunggyi for advanced degrees.
