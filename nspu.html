About the University

Naypyitaw State Polytechnic University (NSPU) is a computer and technology university in Nay Pyi Taw. It was inaugurated as Naypyitaw Technological University in 2022, and later relocated/expanded and upgraded to NSPU in 2024. The university includes 16 academic departments (engineering, computing, and supporting departments) and is designed to combine theory with practical learning.
