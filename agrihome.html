🌾 About SAI Homalin

Established in November 2019 as the **15th State Agricultural Institute** in Myanmar and the **2nd in Sagaing Region**, SAI Homalin serves the agricultural needs of Upper Chindwin valley. Located along the Chindwin River, the institute offers 3-year diploma programs with intensive practical training on river valley agriculture tailored to Sagaing's flood-prone lowlands.

With 70+ acres of demonstration farms focusing on flood-tolerant rice, pulses, and riverbank horticulture, SAI Homalin trains agricultural technicians for northwestern Myanmar's unique riverine agricultural ecosystem.
