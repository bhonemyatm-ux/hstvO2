About the College

သဟကရ ကောလိပ် (မန္တလေး) သည် အထက်မြန်မာပြည်၏ ဦးဆောင်သော သဟကရပညာသင်ကြားရေးဌာန ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၉၃ ခုနှစ်တွင် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး သဟကရမန်နေဂျာများ၊ ခေါင်းဆောင်များနှင့် စီးပွားရေးကျွမ်းကျင်သူများကို လေ့ကျင့်ပေးနေပါသည်။

Co-operative College (Mandalay) was established in 1993 as Upper Myanmar's premier institution dedicated to cooperative education and management training. Located at the base of Yankin Hill on Yan Kin Hill Pat Street, Patheingyi Township, Mandalay Region, the college serves as the cooperative education hub for Mandalay, Sagaing, and Shan States.

Operating under the Ministry of Co-operatives (formerly Department of Co-operatives), CCM offers bachelor's degree programs in cooperative management, business administration, and agriculture cooperation, as well as diploma courses for working cooperative professionals. With 50+ academic staff and 800+ students, CCM has produced 5,000+ graduates serving as cooperative managers, bank officers, agricultural cooperative leaders, and community development specialists across Upper Myanmar. The college maintains close ties with cooperative societies, cooperative banks, and the Ministry of Co-operatives, ensuring strong graduate employability and practical industry relevance.
