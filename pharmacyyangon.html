About the University

ဆေးဝါး တက္ကသိုလ် (ရန်ကုန်) သည် မြန်မာနိုင်ငံ၏ ပထမဆုံးနှင့် အထင်ရှားဆုံးသော ဆေးဝါးပညာသင်ကြားရေးဗဟိုဌာန ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၉၂ ခုနှစ်တွင် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး ၂၀၀၅ ခုနှစ်တွင် တက္ကသိုလ်အဆင့်သို့ မြှင့်တင်ခဲ့ပါသည်။

University of Pharmacy, Yangon (UOPY) traces its origins to 1964 when the Department of Pharmacy was first established under the Institute of Paramedical Sciences at Yangon General Hospital. In January 1992, it was proudly established as the Institute of Pharmacy in the former BOC College building on Thahton Road. The institute moved to its current 19.958-acre campus on Waibargi Road, North Okkalapa in May 2001.

Upgraded to university status in 2005, UOPY now offers Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm), Master of Pharmacy (MPharm), and PhD programs in Pharmacognosy, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutics, and Clinical Pharmacy. The university accepts approximately 150 students annually based on University Entrance Examination scores, making it one of Myanmar's most selective health science institutions.

🏅 Government-Recognized — Only pharmacy degree allowing pharmaceutical practice in Myanmar
