University of Medical Technology, Yangon (UMTY) is Myanmar's first and premier institution for allied health professionals. Established in 1993 as the Institute of Paramedical Science, UMTY trains ~150 students annually across three core specializations: Medical Imaging Technology, Medical Laboratory Technology, and Physiotherapy.

Unlike medical universities, UMTY offers 4-year Bachelor of Medical Technology (B.Med.Tech) degrees with a unique system: students study basic sciences in Year 1, then choose their major based on merit. Clinical training occurs at Yangon General Hospital and other major facilities.

Undergraduate Programs

Choose your medical technology specialization after Year 1

📸

Medical Imaging Technology

B.Med.Tech

4-year degree specializing in X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound. Year 1: Basic sciences. Years 2-4: Advanced imaging physics, radiographic techniques, sectional anatomy. Clinical rotations at Yangon General Hospital (920+ hours).

Duration 4 Years
Major Selection After Year 1 merit ranking
Clinical Hours 920+ hours at 4 hospitals
🧪

Medical Laboratory Technology

B.Med.Tech

4-year degree in clinical laboratory sciences — hematology, microbiology, biochemistry, immunology. Essential for hospital lab operations and diagnostics.

💪

Physiotherapy

B.Med.Tech / BP.Sc

4-year degree in rehabilitation sciences. Covers musculoskeletal, neurological, cardiopulmonary physiotherapy with extensive hands-on clinical practice.
