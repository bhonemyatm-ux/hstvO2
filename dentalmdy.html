About the University

သွားဘက်ဆိုင်ရာ ဆေး တက္ကသိုလ် (မန္တလေး) သည် အထက်မြန်မာပြည်၏ တစ်ခုတည်းသော သွားဘက်ဆိုင်ရာပညာသင်ကြားရေးဗဟိုဌာန ဖြစ်ပါသည်။ ၁၉၉၈ ခုနှစ်တွင် တည်ထောင်ခဲ့ပြီး အထက်မြန်မာပြည်တစ်ဝန်း၏ သွားဆရာဝန်များကို လေ့ကျင့်ပေးနေပါသည်။

University of Dental Medicine, Mandalay (UDMM) was established in 1998 as Myanmar's second dental university, breaking Yangon's 34-year monopoly on dental education. Located at 62nd Street, Chanmyathazi Township, UDMM serves as Upper Myanmar's premier institution for training dentists and dental specialists.

UDMM offers a comprehensive 6-year Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) program, along with Master of Dental Science (MDSc) in eight dental specialties, Doctor of Dental Science (Dr.Dent.Sc), and PhD programs in Oral Medicine, Oral Biological Science, Prosthodontics, Conservative Dentistry, Paediatric Dentistry, Orthodontics, and Periodontology. With approximately 900 students and 196+ teaching staff, UDMM trains about 150 dentists annually, serving Mandalay, Sagaing, Magway, Shan, and Kachin States.

BDS Course Structure

6 years of comprehensive dental education

Year Duration Key Focus Areas
Year 1 1 Year Basic Medical Sciences: Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Dental Anatomy
Year 2 1 Year Oral Sciences: Oral Histology, Oral Pathology, Dental Materials, Microbiology, Pharmacology
Year 3 1 Year Clinical Dentistry I: Operative Dentistry, Prosthodontics, Periodontics, Oral Surgery fundamentals
Year 4 1 Year Clinical Dentistry II: Orthodontics, Pediatric Dentistry, Oral Medicine, Radiology
Year 5 1 Year Advanced Clinical Practice: Comprehensive patient management, specialty rotations, research methodology
Year 6 1 Year Comprehensive Clinical Internship: Full-time patient care in all dental specialties, community postings, final research project
