Scholarship Overview

The ASEAN Scholarship is a prestigious programme administered by Singapore's Ministry of Education (MOE) to nurture outstanding young talents from ASEAN member countries. This scholarship provides exceptional Myanmar students aged 15-18 with a unique opportunity to complete their secondary and pre-university education at top Singapore schools, experiencing world-class education in one of Asia's most advanced education systems.

Scholars study for 4 years (Secondary 3 to Pre-University 2) at selected Singapore secondary schools and junior colleges, leading to the internationally-recognized Singapore-Cambridge GCE A-Level certificate or equivalent qualification. The scholarship covers full school fees, annual allowance with hostel accommodation, settling-in allowance, return economy airfare, and medical benefits. Upon completion, scholars are well-prepared to pursue undergraduate studies at top universities worldwide, with many continuing at NUS, NTU, SMU in Singapore or prestigious universities globally.

✨

What Makes ASEAN Scholarship Special?

This is one of the few fully-funded scholarships for high school students (Grade 9-10 level) to study abroad! You'll experience Singapore's world-renowned education system ranked among the best globally, live in a safe and multicultural environment, develop crucial 21st-century skills, build lifelong friendships with students from across ASEAN, and gain direct pathway to top universities. Singapore's bilingual education policy means you'll strengthen both English and potentially learn Mandarin. Most importantly, you'll receive quality education that opens doors to global opportunities while representing Myanmar on the regional stage!

Scholarship Details

📋

Scholarship Name

ASEAN Scholarship (Singapore Secondary & Pre-University Programme)

  • Administered by Ministry of Education (MOE), Singapore
  • Part of Singapore's commitment to ASEAN regional cooperation
  • Available for students from all 10 ASEAN member countries
  • Separate from ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarships (different programme)
🏛️

Schools & Institutions

Selected Singapore Secondary Schools & Junior Colleges

Scholars are placed at MOE-selected secondary schools for Secondary 3 and 4 (equivalent to Grade 10-11), then progress to junior colleges or Millennia Institute for Pre-University 1 and 2 (equivalent to Grade 12-13). Popular schools include Anderson Secondary, Chung Cheng High (Main), Dunman High, Hwa Chong Institution, Nanyang Girls' High, Raffles Institution, River Valley High, and Victoria School. School placement is determined by MOE based on your performance and preferences.

How to Apply

1

Verify Your Eligibility

Check ALL requirements carefully: birth date (Jan 2, 2008 - Jan 1, 2011), Myanmar citizenship, completed Grade 9+, English proficiency, strong academic record, co-curricular activities. If you don't meet age requirement, wait for next cycle!

Check Official Requirements →
2

Gather Required Documents

Collect birth certificate, Grade 9-10 transcripts and report cards, co-curricular certificates, awards, and achievements. Organize everything neatly for online upload. Scan documents clearly!

3

Write Strong Personal Statement

Craft compelling essay explaining: Why you want to study in Singapore, your academic goals, leadership experiences, how you'll contribute to school community, future plans to contribute to Myanmar. Be genuine, specific, and passionate!

4

Complete Online Application

Visit MOE Singapore website (go.gov.sg/as-myanmar when application opens). Fill out complete online form with accurate information, upload all documents, and submit by May 30, 2025. NO application fee required!

Visit Application Portal →
5

Prepare for Selection Tests

If shortlisted (notified via email), prepare for computer-based tests in Yangon covering English, Mathematics, and General Ability. Review secondary school curriculum, practice English comprehension and writing, solve math problems. Tests assess academic readiness!

6

Attend Selection Interview

If you pass tests, prepare for face-to-face interview in Yangon. Expect questions about: academic interests, motivation for Singapore, leadership experiences, adaptation skills, future goals. Dress professionally, speak confidently, show genuine enthusiasm!

7

Await Results & Prepare for Departure

Successful candidates notified end-September via email. If selected, begin preparations: passport processing, medical check-up, packing, mental preparation for living abroad. Attend pre-departure orientation if provided. Arrive Singapore in November 2025!

📌 Important Application Notes:

  • Completely FREE: No application fee, no agent fees. Never pay anyone for application assistance!
  • Only Shortlisted Notified: If you don't receive selection test invitation, it means you weren't shortlisted
  • Tests in Yangon: All selection activities happen in Myanmar - you don't need to travel to Singapore for application process
  • Computer-Based Tests: Computers/laptops provided at test center - no need to bring your own
  • Age is Non-Negotiable: Birth date requirement cannot be waived under any circumstances
04

Pro Tips for Success

📊

Academic Excellence is Foundation

Maintain consistently high grades across ALL subjects, especially English, Math, and Sciences. Singapore education is rigorous - selection prioritizes students who can handle academic demands. Top students in class have strongest chances!

🗣️

English Proficiency is CRITICAL

All classes in Singapore are conducted in English. Practice reading English books, watching English media, writing essays, speaking daily. Your English skills will be thoroughly tested - weak English = automatic rejection regardless of other strengths!

🎯

Build Strong Co-Curricular Record

Singapore values well-rounded students. Participate actively in sports, clubs, student council, community service, competitions. Leadership roles particularly impressive. Don't just join - CONTRIBUTE and achieve! Collect certificates and documentation.

✍️

Personal Statement: Show Your Character

Don't write generic statements! Share YOUR unique story, specific examples, genuine passion for learning, clear goals. Explain why Singapore specifically appeals to you. Show maturity, adaptability, and commitment to Myanmar's future. Proofread multiple times!

📝

Practice for Selection Tests

Tests cover English (comprehension, grammar, writing), Mathematics (algebra, geometry, problem-solving), and General Ability (logical reasoning, critical thinking). Practice past papers if available, work on speed and accuracy, review Grade 9-10 curriculum thoroughly!

🎤

Interview: Be Genuine & Confident

Interview panel assesses personality, maturity, motivation, and cultural adaptability. Practice common questions, but don't memorize robotic answers. Show enthusiasm, speak clearly, maintain eye contact, demonstrate knowledge about Singapore, ask thoughtful questions!

🌏

Research Singapore Education System

Understand how Singapore schools work: O-Level and A-Level systems, co-curricular activities (CCAs), grading system, school culture. Show you've researched and are genuinely interested in Singapore's unique educational approach. This demonstrates seriousness!

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Prepare Mentally for Independence

You'll live away from family at 15-16 years old. Show maturity, independence, resilience, and adaptability. Selection considers whether you can thrive in boarding environment, manage homesickness, make friends across cultures. Demonstrate emotional readiness!
