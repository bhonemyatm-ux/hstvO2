Feature cards

Use every part of HST with clarity.

ⓘ
01 — Overview

HST Overview

Learn what HST is and how it helps Myanmar students choose universities, careers, and scholarships. Open the main menu, browse the sections, and bookmark the pages you use most.

Explore →
⌂
02 — Universities

Find Your University

Use the Universities section to explore schools, programs, and entry requirements in Myanmar. Filter by city, field of study, and fee range to quickly find options that match your goals.

Browse →
☆
03 — Scholarships

Explore Scholarships

Check the Scholarships page to see current opportunities for students in Myanmar. Read the requirements, deadlines, and links, then save the ones you plan to apply for.

View →
⌁
04 — Career

Plan Your Career Path

The Career Journey section helps you map your skills and interests to suitable careers. Use the steps and examples to think about which subjects and programs will support your long-term plans.

Plan →
◷
05 — Study Mode

Use Study Mode

Study Mode gives you focused tools and recommended routines for exam prep and project work. Turn it on during study hours and use the built-in checklist and timer.

Focus →
♙
06 — Ambassador

Join HST Ambassador

The Student Ambassador program lets you share HST with friends and school communities. Read the details, sign up, and use your unique link to invite others and track your impact.

Join →
✓
07 — Certificates

Verify Your Certificate

Use the Verify Certificate page to check the authenticity of any HST-issued certificate. Enter the Certificate ID at the bottom of the document and review the result on screen.

Verify →
◌
08 — Feedback

Send Feedback & Reports

Found wrong or outdated information? Use the Report page to tell us what is wrong and suggest updates. Schools and organizations can also propose collaboration with HST here.

Report →
