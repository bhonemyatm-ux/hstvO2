About the University

The University of Computer Studies, Meiktila (UCSMTLA) is a government university located in Meiktila, Mandalay Region, Myanmar. Established in 2001 as a Computer College and upgraded to university status in 2007, it serves as a key hub for technology education in Central Myanmar.

Operating under the Ministry of Science and Technology, UCSMTLA offers four-year undergraduate programs including B.C.Sc. (Computer Science) and B.C.Tech. (Computer Technology). These programs train students in Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and network infrastructure.

The university's mission is "Quality Education for Peace, Progress and Change" — developing graduates who are technically skilled and capable of contributing to national modernization. With student-centered teaching, modern computer laboratories, and curricula covering Cybersecurity, Data Mining, and Mobile Development, UCSMTLA bridges academic learning with private sector technology needs.
